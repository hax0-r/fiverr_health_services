Our Company

We thrive on building a healthy and solid community for all individuals.

JTN Health Services, LLC is a healthcare staffing company that specializes in providing qualified and compassionate personnel (CNAs and GNAs) to cater for the care needs of various patient populations. Whether you are looking for staff to assist the elderly, the handicapped, the rehab patients or the special needs patients, JTN Health Services, LLC can help you find the right match for your facility or home care setting.
At JTN Health Services, LLC, we understand that each patient has unique and diverse needs that require personalized and professional care. That is why we carefully screen and select our staff members based on their skills, experience, credentials and background checks. We also provide them with ongoing training and support to ensure they deliver high-quality care that meets or exceeds your expectations.

Our services are flexible and affordable, and we can provide staff for short-term or long-term assignments, as well as on-call or emergency situations. We work closely with you to understand your staffing needs and preferences, and we match you with the best candidates from our pool of qualified and reliable personnel. We also handle all the administrative tasks such as payroll, taxes, insurance and compliance, so you can focus on your core business.

JTN Health Services, LLC is more than just a staffing company. We are a partner in your care delivery and a resource for your staffing solutions. We are committed to providing you with the best personnel and the best service possible. Contact us today to find out how we can help you meet your staffing needs and enhance your patient care.

Our Team

JTN Health Services – Compassionate Care in the Comfort of Your Home

JTN Health Services is a leading provider of quality home healthcare services in the region. We offer a range of services to meet the diverse needs of our clients, from personal care and companionship to skilled nursing and rehabilitation. Our team of experienced and compassionate nurses, home healthcare aides, therapists, and social workers are committed to delivering personalized and professional care to each client in the comfort of their own home. Whether you need short-term or long-term care, we are here to help you achieve your health and wellness goals.
At JTN Health Services, we understand that every client is unique and deserves a customized care plan that suits their specific needs and preferences. That's why we conduct a thorough assessment of each client's medical condition, lifestyle, and family situation before designing a care plan that meets their physical, emotional, and social needs. We also coordinate with your primary care physician and other healthcare providers to ensure continuity of care and optimal outcomes. Our nurses, home healthcare aides, therapists, and social workers are licensed, certified, and trained in the latest best practices and technologies in home healthcare. They are also background-checked, bonded, and insured for your peace of mind.

We are proud to offer a variety of home healthcare services, including:

- Skilled nursing: Our registered nurses (RNs) and licensed practical nurses (LPNs) provide skilled nursing services such as wound care, medication management, IV therapy, diabetes care, catheter care, ostomy care, tracheostomy care, and more.
- Home health aide: Our home health aides (HHAs) assist clients with activities of daily living such as bathing, dressing, grooming, toileting, feeding, transferring, and mobility.
- Physical therapy: Our physical therapists (PTs) help clients improve their strength, balance, coordination, and mobility through exercises, stretches, massage, and other modalities.
- Occupational therapy: Our occupational therapists (OTs) help clients regain their independence and function in their daily activities such as cooking, cleaning, dressing, writing, and more.
- Speech therapy: Our speech-language pathologists (SLPs) help clients improve their communication skills such as speaking, listening, reading, writing, and swallowing.
- Medical social work: Our medical social workers (MSWs) provide counseling and support to clients and their families coping with illness, disability, or loss. They also help clients access community resources and benefits such as financial assistance, transportation, housing, and more.

If you or your loved one needs home healthcare services, contact us today to schedule a free in-home consultation. We are available 24/7 to answer your questions and address your concerns. JTN Health Services is your trusted partner in home healthcare.
